About

Performances. Teachings. Travels.

"Thank you so much for a beautiful performance!! Amazing!!!!!" - Sandie Mefaddi, Bride

Hi, welcome to Sydney Violinist

My name is Angela and I am a violinist based in Sydney. I have recently moved down from Brisbane after living there for 12 years. Prior to that I lived in Qingdao, China. Your might know it as Tsingtao, with its famous Tsingtao Beer.

Even though I've moved states and countries, one thing that's always been with me is my violin. I started playing at the age of four with the Suzuki Method, then went on to compete and win some state and national competitions, completed my LMUS.A. and taught the violin for several years at Allans + Billy Hyde Music.

I am working now in an office job, but my passion for music and in particular the violin only grows stronger. On weekends, I travel to different suburbs with my violin for practising or to perform. You will most likely find me at weddings, functions or other events playing solo or with a quartet

If you have any questions or feedback, don't hesitate to email me at sydneyviolinist@gmail.com. Otherwise, I hope you enjoy reading my blog!
