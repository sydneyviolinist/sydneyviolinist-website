Performances

On weekends, I perform in Sydney and other Australian cities. My favourite performances have included the Paris Notre Dame Cathedral and the Sydney Opera House. Common performances include weddings (including quartets), chamber music, orchestral or solo. To book me, scroll down to the bottom of the page to the "Get In Touch" section.

Teachings

LMus.A certified and with 6 years experience teaching at Allans + Billy Hyde Music, I have taught students aged 4 to 60 - beginners to advanced students.
